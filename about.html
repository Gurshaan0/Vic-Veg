Farm Operations


Vic Veg Farm operates more than 100 Acres across Katandra West in Victoria. Industry leaders in the production of Cabbage, Zucchini and Broccoli, the consistent supply of irrigation water from Melbourne Water means that crops can be produced all year. Our state-of-the-art facilities and equipment include dedicated harvesting and processing equipment, packhouse and cool room storage facilities with the latest farm machinery operating 365 days a year. We utilise the latest genetics across our crop varieties, with our fennel sought for its taste, size and quality.
